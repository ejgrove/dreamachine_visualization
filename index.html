About

Interactive visualization of HDBSCAN-clustered UMAP embeddings of stroboscopically induced visual hallucinations from Dreamachine, a large-scale public-engagement stroboscopic light experience.

Adapted from “A Large-Scale Computer-Vision Mapping of the Geometric Structures of Stroboscopically-Induced Visual Hallucinations” - Grove et al., 2026.

This visualisation uses Dreamachine participant drawings (2022), courtesy of Collective Act. Dreamachine (https://dreamachine.world) was produced by Collective Act and presented as part of UNBOXED: Creativity in the UK.

The images remain the copyright of Collective Act and are used here with permission for research visualisation. The drawing images may not be reproduced separately from this visualisation without the permission of Collective Act.

The visualisation code is derived from the ScatterGL demo, licensed under the Apache 2.0 Licence.
