About

Interactive visualization of HDBSCAN-clustered UMAP embeddings of stroboscopically induced visual hallucinations from Dreamachine, a large-scale public-engagement stroboscopic light experience.

Adapted from “A Large-Scale Computer-Vision Mapping of the Geometric Structures of Stroboscopically-Induced Visual Hallucinations” - Grove et al., 2026.

Code is derived from ScatterGL demo - Apache-2.0 license.
